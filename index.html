The Republic of Sudan, or Sudan for short, is a country in Northeast Africa. It is the third largest country in Africa, as well as in the Arab world. Its capital is Khartoum and it is bordered on the north by Egypt, on the east by Ethiopia and Eritrea, on the south by South Sudan, on the Central African Republic on the southwest, by Chad on the west and by Libya on the northwest. Its official languages ​​are Arabic and English
